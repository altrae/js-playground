Rotating Images

Rotating images are images that rotate. I like rotating images because I like to look at images, but I am too lazy to flip the pages.

Rotating Images Poem

Ro-ro-rotating images,
gently through a stream.
Merrily, merrily, merrily, merrily.
Each image is but a dream.
